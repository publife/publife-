How It Works

Kansas City Metropolitan Area
Financial assistance designed to be fast, dignified, and accountable.

What PubLife Covers

PubLife provides fast, efficient, and confidential financial assistance to hospitality workers in our community. This includes emergency room visits, urgent care, pharmacy costs, mental health services, and substance abuse treatment.

Stewardship & Accountability

PubLife is built to operate as a true nonprofit, with a strong focus on directing the majority of funds toward those in need. Our model targets allocating 80% to 85% of all funds raised directly to medical grants, while keeping operating costs low and intentional.

How Funds Are Distributed

When an application is approved, funds are distributed directly to the provider or institution associated with the medical expense whenever possible. This ensures that support is used as intended and reaches the right place without delay.

Oversight & Fairness

PubLife operates as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit with oversight from a Board of Directors. The Executive Director reviews applications and has the authority to approve or deny requests based on established criteria.

To protect the integrity of the process, a strict conflict of interest policy is in place. Every application is handled with consistency, fairness, and respect.

Our Commitment

At its core, PubLife is built to respond when it matters most, providing timely and dignified support to the people who keep our communities running every day.
