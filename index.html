About PubLife

PubLife exists to serve the ones who serve us.

What We Do

PubLife provides financial assistance to hospitality workers facing unexpected medical hardship.

How It Works

Applicants submit documentation for review. Approved requests are paid directly to providers whenever possible.

Guidelines

Maximum grant: $2,500
One grant per 12-month period
Funds are allocated based on availability

How We Sustain This Work

Through community events, local partnerships, and direct contributions, PubLife is sustained by the very community it exists to serve.

Our Commitment

We are committed to operating with transparency, dignity, and efficiency.

Additional Information

While we strive to assist as many individuals as possible, assistance is not guaranteed and is dependent on available funds and eligibility review.
