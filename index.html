About PubLife

PubLife exists to serve the ones who serve us.

What We Do

PubLife provides financial assistance to hospitality workers facing unexpected medical hardship.

How It Works

Applicants submit documentation for review. Approved requests are paid directly to providers whenever possible.

Guidelines

Maximum grant: $2,500
One grant per 12-month period
Funds are allocated based on availability

How We Sustain This Work

Through community events, local partnerships, and direct contributions.
