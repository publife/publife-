Apply for Assistance

Kansas City Metropolitan Area
Seeking help can be difficult. PubLife exists to make that process as simple, respectful, and dignified as possible.

Before You Apply

If you are facing a medical expense and need support, you can apply here. All applications are handled with care, confidentiality, and respect.

You do not need to have everything prepared before applying. Share what you can now. If additional information is needed, we will follow up directly.

What to Expect

Every submission is reviewed within 24 hours. Our goal is to respond quickly so you are not left waiting during a difficult time.

Please submit only one application per request. If your situation changes or you need to provide additional information, you can update us directly.

Privacy & Confidentiality

All information submitted is kept strictly confidential and used only for the purpose of evaluating your request.

You are not alone in this. We are here to help.
